Special Tests

Our office is equipped with the latest in technology to provide a wide array of services to our patients.

In-office laser surgery is performed for a variety of eye conditions including glaucoma and diabetic retinopathy. Computerized visual fields are instrumental in the diagnosis of various eye diseases, especially glaucoma. Optic Nerve Tomography is used for the earliest possible detection of glaucoma. Computerized corneal topography provides us with state of the art analysis of the cornea.
